Rangpur

Nestled in the northern region of Bangladesh, Rangpur is a city of historical charm and natural beauty, often celebrated as the "Gateway to the North." Rich in cultural heritage, Rangpur boasts landmarks like the stately Tajhat Palace, a splendid testament to Mughal-inspired architecture, and the serene Begum Rokeya Memorial, honoring the legacy of the renowned social reformer. The lush Ramsagar National Park, home to the largest man-made lake in Bangladesh, offers a tranquil retreat amidst nature. Rangpur's vibrant markets, including Vinnojagat, and its unique traditional crafts showcase the creativity of its people. With its picturesque landscapes, historical significance, and warm hospitality, Rangpur offers a delightful experience for visitors seeking both relaxation and a touch of history.
